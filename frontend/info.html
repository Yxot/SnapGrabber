About SnapGrabber

SnapGrabber is a free, open-source video downloader inspired by Cobalt. Download videos from your favorite platforms in the best quality, with or without audio, and no watermarks.

Supported Platforms

  • TikTok
  • Instagram
  • YouTube
  • Twitter
  • Reddit
  • SoundCloud
  • Vimeo
  • and more...

Credits

  • Powered by Cobalt
  • Frontend: HTML, Tailwind CSS, JavaScript
  • Backend: Python (FastAPI)

Privacy

SnapGrabber does not track users or store any personal data. All downloads are processed in real-time and are not logged or cached.
